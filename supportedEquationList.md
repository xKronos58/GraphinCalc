  - `v = √ {(2 * m * g) / (d * A * C)}`
- ## Speed

  - `S = ∆d / ∆t`
- ## Acceleration

  - `A = ∆v / ∆t`

  - `F = MAn`
  - `QvB = mv² / r`

- ## Density 

  - `D = w / v`
- ## Weight 

  - `W = m * gs`

- ## Work Done

  - `W = F' * D * cos(θ)`
  - `F' = M * A`
- ## Kinetic Energy

  - `KE = ½ * m * v²`
